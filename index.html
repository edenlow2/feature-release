Organisation Management Enhancements

Product: Pantas Enterprise | Release Date: June 16, 2025

Highlights

Interactive Hierarchy View

Visualize and manage your entire company structure from a single, interactive map.

Bulk Team Creation

Accelerate setup by creating multiple teams at once via file upload.

Centralized Administration

Manage companies, teams, and business entities from dedicated modules.

Detailed Enhancements

Company Management

What's New

  • New interactive hierarchy view of your organization.
  • Add new sub-companies directly within the hierarchy.
  • View, edit, or remove individual companies.
  • Manage teams assigned to a specific company.

Why It Matters

Provides a clear, visual map of your corporate structure, making it easier to navigate complex relationships between business units.

Availability

Available now to all users with administrative permissions.

Limitations

Removing a company is blocked if it has linked sub-companies, teams, or users to prevent accidental data loss.

Teams Management

What's New

  • New centralized module for all teams.
  • Create multiple teams at once via bulk upload.
  • View a comprehensive list of all teams.
  • Edit or delete individual teams as needed.

Why It Matters

Saves significant administrative time by simplifying team setup and management, especially for large organizations.

Availability

Available now to all users with administrative permissions.

Entities Management

What's New

  • Dedicated module for managing business entities.
  • Add, edit, or remove individual entities.
  • View a complete list of all entities.
  • Assign entities during company creation.

Why It Matters

Streamlines asset administration by providing a single, consolidated view to manage all business entities, ensuring proper governance.

Availability

Available now. This module is accessible only to administrators of the top-level company.
